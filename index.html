RICH vs WEALTH:

What is the true meaning?

The goal isn't to be a "millionaire " or to be "rich" for the cash, or materialistic things. The DOLLAR is just a piece of paper, the dollar has no real value, being "rich" does not mean having alot of money and/or cash to me, BEING rich to me is FREEDOM, being comfortable, and most importantly HEALTH, health is WEALTH! Being healthy is rich!. Rich is a mindset, if you have a family that loves you, a roof over your head, clean water, clean clothes, food to eat, and your healthy, you are RICH! Because not every one has those things we take for GRANTED, not everyone has a family, not everyone has a roof over their head, unfortunately. Life is HARD for everyone, we all go through heartaches and pains. Whether your "rich" or "poor", we all go through struggles in life. And no one is immortal, we all DIE, AND OUR CASKETS WILL BE THE SAME SIZE no matter your what your "status" is. That is why I believe in being HUMBLE in life. And being GRATEFUL for the things people take for GRANTED!

GRATITUDE


That's what makes LIFE so beautiful and Precious. These are things people take for granted, I thank God every day for my family, being healthy, and most importantly for God waking me up every morning. Because tomorrow is NOT promised!

FREEDOM
